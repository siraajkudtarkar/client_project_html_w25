Academic Advising & Support

UMSI academic advisors are here to help you with course selection, degree planning, and career goals. Whether you need guidance on specialization choices or have questions about university policies, our advisors are ready to support you.

  • Connect with an Advisor - Book an appointment with an advisor to discuss academic and career planning.
  • Help! Who Do I Contact? - Find the right contact for specific academic, financial, or personal concerns.
  • Faculty Office Hours - Meet with your professors during their office hours to discuss coursework and research opportunities.

MSI Degree Requirements & Course Planning

Understanding your degree requirements and planning your coursework effectively is crucial for graduation. Here are some key resources:
