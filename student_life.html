Student Life: Basic Needs

Ensuring fair and equitable access to essential resources, including food, housing, healthcare, and financial assistance, is a top priority for student success. Below, you’ll find categorized resources to help you navigate challenges and stay focused on your education.

Basic Needs Resources by Category

  • Critical Incident & Management
  • Financial Support
  • Food Programs
  • Healthcare Assistance
  • Housing & Transporation
  • Other Resources

Critical Incident Support & Conflict Management

Unexpected incidents and conflicts can create emotional, financial, and academic stress. Whether you're facing a crisis, dealing with personal conflicts, or seeking mediation, U-M offers confidential support and resources to help you navigate challenging situations.

  • Emergency financial assistance for unexpected crises
  • Conflict resolution and mediation services
  • Mental health support for crisis management

Finance

Financial stability is essential for student well-being. U-M offers resources to help you manage tuition, living expenses, and unexpected financial hardships.

  • Emergency aid and short-term loans
  • Financial counseling and budgeting resources
  • On-campus and community employment opportunities

Food

Access to nutritious food is crucial for your academic success. If you’re facing food insecurity, there are several resources available to help you.

  • Maize & Blue Cupboard - Provides free groceries and personal care items for students.
  • Free & Low-Cost Meal Programs - Local organizations and campus initiatives offer affordable meal options.
  • Grocery Shopping Assistance - Resources for students without transportation.

Health Care

Your physical and mental well-being are key to your success. U-M provides comprehensive healthcare options, including primary care, mental health counseling, and wellness programs.

  • UHS (University Health Services) for routine checkups and urgent care
  • CAPS (Counseling and Psychological Services) for mental health support
  • Assistance with securing health insurance

Housing & Transportation

Whether you need help finding housing, navigating transportation options, or dealing with unexpected housing concerns, U-M offers resources to support your needs.

  • Off-Campus Housing Search - Find apartments and housing options near campus.
  • University Housing Assistance - Support for students in need of temporary or emergency housing.
  • Transportation Services - Free bus and ride-sharing services for students.

More Resources

For a full list of available student support services, visit the U-M Basic Needs website for additional information on financial aid, emergency grants, food assistance, and more.
